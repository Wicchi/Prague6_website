"Official" Homepage of the Prague 6 District

Welcome to the main page for the Prague 6 district where you can find out anything you might need to know that is happening in your neighbourhood right now.
This district is located in the beautiful city of Prague, Czech Republic, and it is most known for the great student community in the area! It consists of the municipal districts Lysolaje, Nebusice, Predni Kopanina and Suchdol. Prague 6 is also the largest municipal district in Prague with 100 000 inhabitants and 41km2.
Here you can find out the latest news, traffic restrictions, best events, etc. Feel free to browse through the various tabs and explore everything that Prague 6 can bring you! You can also contact us and ask us anything which might be of your interest, or meet us in person.
